Configurable AI customer assistant

Configurable AI assistant for customer support, lead qualification, RAG answers and CRM-style workflows.

How it works

Configure your business profile → add your knowledge-base files → ingest them → connect an OpenAI-compatible LLM (or run in offline mock mode). The assistant answers, qualifies leads, and escalates to a human when asked.
