# FAQ

> Sample knowledge base. Replace with your own FAQs and re-ingest.

**How quickly can we start?**
Onboarding usually takes 1–2 weeks from a signed agreement.

**Do you require long contracts?**
We ask for an initial 3-month commitment, then it's month-to-month.

**Is ad spend included in your fees?**
No. Management fees and ad spend are separate; platforms bill ad spend directly.

**Can you guarantee results?**
No reputable studio guarantees specific results. We set realistic targets and
report transparently.

**Who owns the ad accounts and data?**
You do. We work inside your accounts and you keep full ownership.

**How do we get a custom quote?**
Book a discovery call; custom retainers typically start around $10,000/month.
