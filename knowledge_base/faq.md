# NovaGrowth Agency – FAQ

> Synthetic FAQ for a fictional agency.

**How quickly can we start?**
Onboarding usually takes 1–2 weeks from a signed agreement.

**Do you require long contracts?**
We ask for an initial 3-month commitment, then it's month-to-month.

**Is ad spend included in your fees?**
No. Management fees and ad spend are separate; platforms bill ad spend directly.

**Which industries do you work with?**
E-commerce, local services, and early-stage B2B SaaS. We don't take enterprise accounts needing dedicated 24/7 teams.

**Can you guarantee results?**
No reputable agency guarantees specific results. We set realistic targets and report transparently.

**How do we get a custom quote?**
Book a discovery call. Custom retainers start around $10,000/month.

**Who owns the ad accounts and data?**
You do. We work inside your accounts and you retain full ownership.

**What happens if we want to pause?**
After the initial commitment you can pause or cancel with 30 days' notice.
