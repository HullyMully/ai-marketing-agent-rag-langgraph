# Onboarding

> Sample knowledge base. Replace with your own onboarding process and re-ingest.

Onboarding takes 1–2 weeks from a signed agreement to first activity.

## Step 1 — Kickoff (Day 1–2)
A 60-minute call to align on goals, KPIs, audience and brand guidelines. We
collect access to ad, analytics and any creative assets.

## Step 2 — Access & tracking (Day 2–5)
We verify or set up conversion tracking (GA4, pixels, server-side where relevant)
and audit existing campaigns.

## Step 3 — Strategy & plan (Day 5–8)
We deliver a channel plan: budgets, audiences, creative angles and a 90-day
roadmap for your review and approval.

## Step 4 — Build & launch (Day 8–12)
Campaigns, audiences and creatives are built and launched; reporting dashboards
go live.

## What we need from you
- Admin access to ad and analytics accounts
- Brand assets (logos, fonts, product photos)
- A primary point of contact for approvals
