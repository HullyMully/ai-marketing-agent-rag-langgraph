# NovaGrowth Agency – Client Onboarding

> Synthetic onboarding process for a fictional agency.

Onboarding takes 1–2 weeks from signed agreement to first campaign activity.

## Step 1 – Kickoff (Day 1–2)
A 60-minute kickoff call to align on goals, KPIs, target audience, and brand guidelines. We collect access to ad accounts, analytics, and any existing creative assets.

## Step 2 – Access & Tracking Setup (Day 2–5)
We verify or set up conversion tracking (GA4, pixels, server-side where relevant), confirm billing on ad platforms, and audit existing campaigns.

## Step 3 – Strategy & Plan (Day 5–8)
We deliver a channel plan: budgets, audiences, creative angles, and a 90-day roadmap. Client reviews and approves.

## Step 4 – Build & Launch (Day 8–12)
Campaigns, audiences, and creatives are built and launched. Content calendars are drafted for content/social retainers.

## Step 5 – Reporting Cadence
Reporting dashboards go live. Retainer clients get bi-weekly or weekly check-ins depending on their package.

## What We Need From You
- Admin access to ad and analytics accounts
- Brand assets (logos, fonts, product photos)
- A primary point of contact for approvals
